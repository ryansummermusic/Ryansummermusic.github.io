File Delivery for Mixing

Thank you for trusting me with your art!
To ensure a smooth workflow and preserve the integrity of your work,
please adhere to the following guidelines when delivering files


Reference Mix

Please make sure to provide a WAV file of the latest mix for referencing purposes.


DAW Session

(Pro Tools, Ableton & Logic only)

- Please freeze all tracks/busses/sends with active plugins.
- For Ableton/Logic sessions, please export any non-freezable  busses & sends with active plugins.

OR



WAV Multi-Tracks

- Sample Rate: Same as source
- Bit Depth: 32-bit (Float)
(24-bit is acceptable if no digital clipping occurs at 0dB)





Note: Before exporting, please ensure no tracks exceed 0dBFS, to maintain mix integrity.

How to export your WAV Multi-Tracks

For Vocals

✅ A Processed/Wet Stems folder:
(Leaving all processing/volume/automation on and 
additionally, exporting any grouped tracks/buses/sends)

✅ An Unprocessed/Dry Stems folder:
(Leaving only pitch/time correction - or any other elements you wish to keep intact).

For Instrumental

✅ A Processed/Wet Stems folder:
(Maintaining all processing/automation/volume
balance as heard in the rough mix).

✅ An Unprocessed/Dry Stems folder (Optional):
(Feel free to include Kick, Bass, or any other instruments if unsure of any processing).

For any questions or assistance, feel free to contact me.
I'm looking forward to help bring your vision to life!
